FOOD HABITS

food and nutrition tips during self-quarantine

  1. Make a plan - take only what you need
  2. Prepare home-cooked meals
  3. Take advantage of food delivery options
  4. Follow safe food handling practices
  5. Limit your salt intake
  6. Limit your sugar intake
  7. Limit your fat intake
  8. Consume enough fibre
  9. Stay hydrated
  10. Avoid alcohol or at least reduce your alcohol consumption
  11. Enjoy family meals
  12. Long-lasting fresh fruits and vegetables
  13. WHO recommends consuming a minimum of 400 g (i.e. 5 portions) of fruits and vegetables per day. Citrus fruits like oranges, clementines and grapefruit are good options, as well as bananas and apples,
  15. Root vegetables such as carrots, turnips and beets, as well as vegetables like cabbage, broccoli and cauliflower are relatively nonperishable. Garlic, ginger and onions are also great options to keep at home, as they can be used to add flavour to a variety of meals.
